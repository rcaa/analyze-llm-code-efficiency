ID: 1037
Title: Valid Boomerang
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/valid-boomerang/
Status: OK

solution: ```python
def isBoomerang(points):
    x1, y1 = points[0]
    x2, y2 = points[1]
    x3, y3 = points[2]
    return (y2 - y1) * (x3 - x2) != (y3 - y2) * (x2 - x1)
```

efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(1)
space complexity: O(1)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: This solution uses the concept of the cross product of two vectors to determine if the three points are a boomerang. If the cross product is not zero, then the points are not in a straight line, and thus they form a boomerang. This solution has a constant time complexity and space complexity, making it efficient for this problem.
