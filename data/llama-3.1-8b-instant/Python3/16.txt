ID: 1984
Title: Minimum Difference Between Highest and Lowest of K Scores
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/minimum-difference-between-highest-and-lowest-of-k-scores/
Status: OK

solution: `return max(nums) - min(nums)`
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(1)
space complexity: O(1)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: This solution works by directly returning the difference between the maximum and minimum values in the array, which is the minimum possible difference. This approach has a time complexity of O(1) and space complexity of O(1) as it only involves a constant number of operations and does not use any additional space.
