Title: Check If String Is a Prefix of Array
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/check-if-string-is-a-prefix-of-array/
Status: OK

solution: `def isPrefixString(s, words): for i in range(1, len(words) + 1): prefix = ''.join(words[:i]) if prefix == s: return True return False`
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(n*m) where n is the number of words and m is the maximum length of a word
space complexity: O(m) where m is the maximum length of a word
energy implications: LOW
explanation: This solution iterates over the list of words, concatenating the first i words to form a prefix. It checks if the prefix matches the given string s. This approach has a high efficiency as it only requires a single pass through the list of words. However, it may not be suitable for very large inputs due to its time complexity.
