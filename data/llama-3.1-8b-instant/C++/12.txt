ID: 1037
Title: Valid Boomerang
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/valid-boomerang/
Status: OK

solution: 
```cpp
bool isBoomerang(vector<vector<int>>& points) {
    return (points[1][1] - points[0][1]) * (points[2][0] - points[1][0]) != (points[2][1] - points[1][1]) * (points[1][0] - points[0][0]);
}
```
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(1)
space complexity: O(1)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: This solution uses the shoelace formula to calculate the area of the triangle formed by the three points. If the area is not zero, then the points are not in a straight line and form a boomerang. This solution has a constant time complexity and space complexity, making it efficient for this problem.
