ID: 1037
Title: Valid Boomerang
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/valid-boomerang/
Status: OK

solution: 
```python
def isBoomerang(points):
  return (points[1][0] - points[0][0]) * (points[2][1] - points[0][1]) != (points[1][1] - points[0][1]) * (points[2][0] - points[0][0])

```
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(1)
space complexity: O(1)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: The solution calculates the determinant of the matrix formed by the three points. If the determinant is non-zero, the points are not collinear and form a boomerang.  This approach is efficient as it involves constant-time operations.
