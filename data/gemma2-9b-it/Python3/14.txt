Title: Concatenation of Array

solution: 
```python
def extend_array(nums):
  return nums + nums

```
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(n)
space complexity: O(n)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: The solution concatenates the input array with itself, effectively doubling its size. This is a straightforward approach with linear time and space complexity.
