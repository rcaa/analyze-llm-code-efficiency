Title: Minimum Difference Between Highest and Lowest of K Scores
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/minimum-difference-between-highest-and-lowest-of-k-scores/
Status: OK

solution: 
```python
def minimize_difference(nums, k):
  nums.sort()
  return nums[k-1] - nums[0] 
```
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(N log N)
space complexity: O(1)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: Sorts the array and returns the difference between the k-th smallest and the smallest element. This approach leverages sorting for efficient finding of the minimum and maximum values within the selected k elements.
