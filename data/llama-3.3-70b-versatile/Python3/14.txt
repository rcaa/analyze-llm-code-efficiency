Title: Concatenation of Array
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/concatenation-of-array/
Status: OK

solution: `ans = nums + nums`
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(n)
space complexity: O(n)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: The solution utilizes Python's built-in list concatenation feature to create the desired array `ans` by concatenating the input array `nums` with itself, resulting in a time and space complexity of O(n) and high efficiency due to the simplicity of the operation.
