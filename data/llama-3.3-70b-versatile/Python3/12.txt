Title: Valid Boomerang
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/valid-boomerang/
Status: OK

solution: 
```python
def isBoomerang(points):
    return (points[1][1] - points[0][1]) * (points[2][0] - points[1][0]) != (points[2][1] - points[1][1]) * (points[1][0] - points[0][0])
```
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(1)
space complexity: O(1)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: The solution uses the cross product of vectors to determine if three points are collinear. If the cross product is non-zero, the points are not collinear and form a boomerang. This approach has a constant time complexity and space complexity, making it efficient for this problem.
