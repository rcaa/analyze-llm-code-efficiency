Title: Number of Valid Words for Each Puzzle
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/number-of-valid-words-for-each-puzzle/
Status: OK

solution: Preprocess each word into a bitmask and group them by their characters. For each puzzle, check how many words' bitmasks are subsets of the puzzle's bitmask and contain the puzzle's first character.

efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(W * C + P * W/C), where W is the number of words, C is the average number of unique characters per word, and P is the number of puzzles.
space complexity: O(W * C)
energy implications: LOW

explanation: The solution preprocesses words into bitmasks and groups them by their characters. For each puzzle, it efficiently counts valid words using bitwise operations, ensuring optimal performance even for large inputs.
