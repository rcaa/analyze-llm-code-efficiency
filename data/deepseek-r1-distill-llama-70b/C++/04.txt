ID: 1799
Title: Maximize Score After N Operations
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/maximize-score-after-n-operations/
Status: OK

solution: Use dynamic programming with bitmasking to explore all possible pairings, maximizing the score by considering each operation's contribution based on the gcd of the chosen pair.

efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(2^n * n^2)
space complexity: O(2^n)
energy implications: LOW

explanation: The solution employs a DP approach where each state represents a subset of used elements. For each state, all possible pairs are considered, and the score is updated to maximize the total. This ensures that all possible pairings are evaluated efficiently, leveraging the problem's constraints to keep computations feasible.
