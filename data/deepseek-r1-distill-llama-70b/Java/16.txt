Title: Minimum Difference Between Highest and Lowest of K Scores
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/minimum-difference-between-highest-and-lowest-of-k-scores/
Status: OK

solution: 
Sort the array and use a sliding window of size k to find the minimum difference between the maximum and minimum in each window.

efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(n log n)
space complexity: O(1)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: Sorting the array allows us to efficiently find the smallest range by checking consecutive elements, ensuring optimal performance.
