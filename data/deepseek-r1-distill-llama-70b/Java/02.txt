Title: Number of Valid Words for Each Puzzle
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/number-of-valid-words-for-each-puzzle/
Status: OK

solution: Preprocess words by their first character and store their bitmasks. For each puzzle, compute its bitmask and count words starting with the puzzle's first character whose bitmask is a subset of the puzzle's bitmask.

efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(N * L + P * Q), where N is words count, L is word length, P is puzzles count, Q is average words per first char
space complexity: O(N)
energy implications: LOW

explanation: Groups words by first character and uses bitmasks for quick subset checks, reducing redundant computations.
