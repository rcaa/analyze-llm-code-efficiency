ID: 3525
Title: Find X Value of Array II
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/find-x-value-of-array-ii/
Status: OK

solution: 
For each query, update the array, then compute the prefix products modulo k for the subarray starting at the given index, and count how many times the specified x-value occurs.

efficiency: LOW
time complexity: O(n * q)
space complexity: O(1)
energy implications: HIGH
explanation: This approach involves iterating through the subarray for each query, making it inefficient for large inputs but straightforward to implement.
