ID: 2418
Title: Sort the People
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/sort-the-people/
Status: OK

solution: def solve(names, heights):
    combined = list(zip(names, heights))
    combined.sort(key=lambda x: -x[1])
    return [name for name, _ in combined]

efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(n log n)
space complexity: O(n)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: Pairs names and heights, sorts by height in descending order, then extracts names. Efficient sorting with minimal overhead.
