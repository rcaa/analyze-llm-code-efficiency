Title: Concatenation of Array
URL: https://leetcode.com/problems/concatenation-of-array/
Status: OK

solution: def concatenate(nums):
    return nums + nums
efficiency: HIGH
time complexity: O(n)
space complexity: O(n)
energy implications: LOW
explanation: The solution directly concatenates the input array with itself, achieving O(n) time and space complexity by leveraging Python's list concatenation operator. This approach is optimal as it requires no additional processing beyond copying elements twice.
